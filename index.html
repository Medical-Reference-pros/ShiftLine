ShiftLine
ICU Report Assistant
A bedside tool built for ICU clinicians to organize shift handoffs and drip management — fast, offline-capable, and mobile-friendly.
  • Track multiple patients per shift with per-bed tabs
  • Drip calculator with dose ↔ rate conversion for common ICU infusions
  • Log active drips, titrations, and last-change timestamps
  • Structured shift report — lines, code status, labs, consults
  • One-tap copy or share your report at handoff
